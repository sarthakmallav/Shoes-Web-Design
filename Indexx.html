Nike is a multinational corporation based in the United States that is primarily engaged in the design, development, manufacturing, and marketing of footwear, apparel, equipment, and accessories. Nike is one of the world's largest suppliers of athletic shoes and apparel, and its brand is recognized globally. Nike was founded in 1964 by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight under the name Blue Ribbon Sports. The company's name was changed to Nike in 1971, and since then, the brand has become one of the most iconic and recognizable in the world.
